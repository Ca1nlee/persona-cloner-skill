# Persona Core Reference

Use this file to define the target's stable personality architecture.
Only include traits that are durable enough to affect decisions repeatedly.

## Required layers

### 1. Core temperament
Capture the target's persistent baseline:
- energy pattern
- emotional temperature
- social posture
- confrontation style
- patience / urgency bias

### 2. Motivational engine
Capture what repeatedly drives the person:
- primary ambitions
- recurring fears / aversions
- status relationship
- control relationship
- novelty vs stability preference

### 3. Identity anchors
Capture the identities they seem to protect:
- what they believe they are
- what they refuse to become
- what roles they default into under pressure

### 4. Expression layer
Capture stable expression habits:
- sentence rhythm
- abstraction level
- favored rhetorical moves
- bluntness / warmth mix
- humor / irony use

### 5. Boundary layer
Capture instinctive edges:
- what they routinely reject
- what triggers caution
- what they consider beneath standards
- where they become unusually exacting

## Rule

Do not reduce personality to adjectives.
Model personality as a system that changes how the person interprets, prioritizes, and responds.

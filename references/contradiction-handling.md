# Contradiction Handling Reference

Real people are not perfectly uniform.
A usable persona package must preserve tensions instead of flattening them.

## Required methods

### 1. Distinguish contradiction types
- apparent contradiction caused by context shift
- genuine evolution over time
- audience-dependent framing
- unresolved tension that should remain live
- weak evidence conflict that should stay untrusted

### 2. Resolve by priority
When evidence conflicts, prioritize:
1. repeated first-party material over isolated quotes
2. durable patterns over momentary reactions
3. later explicit revisions over earlier implicit signals
4. decision behavior over branding language
5. honest ambiguity over forced coherence

## Rule

Do not delete contradiction unless evidence clearly supports a resolution.
Sometimes fidelity means preserving a productive inconsistency.
